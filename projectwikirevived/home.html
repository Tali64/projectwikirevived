On September 1, 2021, Google discontinued the legacy version of its site-building tool Google Sites (known as Classic Sites), restricting site creation to the new version of Google Sites (known as New Sites). Unfortunately, this meant the death of Project Wiki, one of the many templates of Classic Sites that was not included in New Sites; although the template itself was still available for viewing after Classic Sites was deprecated, as of August 31, 2023, this is no longer the case (fortunately, an archive exists here).

Project Wiki Revived aims to bring back the Project Wiki template with a modern look and feel, while still remaining faithful to the original. If you're nostalgic about Classic Sites and want the old templates back, this project is for you!

Here is a table comparing Project Wiki with Project Wiki Revived:

Project Wiki Project Wiki Revived
Looks outdated Looks fresh and modern
No support from Google Potential for community support
Limited customization Complete creative control
Exclusive to Classic Sites Can be used anywhere, with a little setup

Other templates included with Classic Sites can be found here.
